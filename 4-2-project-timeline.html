4.1. CONTENT CURATION

INTRODUCTION

Content curation is important because it helps us find and organize the huge amount of information available today. By selecting reliable and relevant content, we can understand topics more easily and avoid confusion or misinformation. It also saves time and allows us to focus on what really matters, making learning more effective and meaningful.
