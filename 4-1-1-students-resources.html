DIGITAL SAFETY

Digital safety is very important in our daily lives because we use the internet for learning, communication, and fun. It helps us protect our personal information, such as passwords, photos, and private messages. By following simple rules, like using strong passwords, not sharing personal data, and being careful with unknown links, we can stay safe online. In this infographic, you will find easy tips to help you use the internet in a smart and secure way.
