TRAVEL GUIDE PROJECT

This project is about creating a travel guide for a route in an English-speaking country. You will choose places to visit and give useful information for travelers. You will also include ideas to travel in a sustainable way, helping to protect the environment and support local communities. Your guide should be clear, interesting, and easy to understand.
